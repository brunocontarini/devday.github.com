About DEVDAY 2014

The DevIsland community is organizing DEVDAY again, an event for software developers.

In its fifth edition, the DEVDAY 2014 will bring together approximately 450 developers.
We will have professionals recognized by the national market that will share their knowledge in 20 lectures that make up the event agenda.
